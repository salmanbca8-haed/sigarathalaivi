சிகரத்தலைவி

Sigarathalaivi is a women entrepreneurship and retail empowerment initiative by FU-GLIDE Innovation Intelligence Network, building confident, skilled, and financially independent women entrepreneurs across Tamil Nadu through training, retail opportunities, and leadership development.

Our Three-Division Model lets every woman grow at her own level:

  • Graduate Leadership Division — trains business leaders and franchise partners.
  • Retail Operations Division — builds skills in billing, inventory, and store management.
  • Community Division — supports grassroots women with financial literacy and self-employment.

Vision: To build one of India's most trusted women entrepreneurship networks.

Mission: To turn aspiring women into successful, financially independent entrepreneurs through training, mentorship, and digital empowerment.

Motto: "One Uniform • One Identity • One Movement."
